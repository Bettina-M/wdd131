Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to provide insights on successful marriage from a Christian perspective.This will help couples or those planning to get married be able to know the importance of eternal marriage, common challenges christian couples face and ways in which they can make it even more better. The material on different mariage topics will be selected from talks from Latter Day Saint church leaders found in the gospel library.

Audience

My primary audience is young single adults planning on getting married and also married couples. The website will have colors and layouts that will make it more appealing for the reader.
